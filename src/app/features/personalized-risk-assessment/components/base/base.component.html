How Safe is my area?

When answering this question, it is important to first clearly define some keywords:

  • An accident is an unplanned event that results in loss of property or life.

  • A hazard is something that has the potential to cause harm, such as floods, landslides, storm surges, and earthquakes.

  • A risk is the likelihood and the severity of negative occurrence such as injury, ill-health, damage, and loss resulting from a hazard.
