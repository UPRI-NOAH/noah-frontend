Flood Safety Tips

Before:

  • Stay informed on local news.

  • Prepare an emergency bag with food, drugs, documents, flashlights, pet items.

  • Know how to evacuate and safe alternative routes.

  • Leave before flooding starts.

  • In flood prone areas, keep useful items at home (sandbags, ladder, rope, etc).

    • During:

  • Disconnect electricity and gas.

  • Don't walk or driver in flood water.

  • Get to a higher ground.

  • Follow evacuation orders.

  • Free cattle and lead animals to a safer place.

    • After:

  • Avoid contact with Flood water and swimming.

  • Don't touch power lines.

  • Don't go home to disaster areas until it is declared safe.

  • Communicate with your family that you are safe.

  • When back home, clean and disinfect surfaces and items.
