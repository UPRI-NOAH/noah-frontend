Landslide Safety Tips

WHEN YOU’RE INSIDE A BUILDING...

  • DROP - duck or drop on the floor

  • COVER - take cover under a desk or sturdy furniture

  • HOLD - hold onto the sturdy desk


  • Stay AWAY from glass windows, shelves, and other heavy objects

  • Stay ALERT and watch for falling objects

  • Stay PUT until the shaking stops. Once it's safe, calmly proceed to the nearest exit.


  • In evacuating, take the stars. DO NOT use the elevator to avoid getting trapped in case of power failure.

    • WHEN YOU’RE OUTSIDE A BUILDING...

  • Move to an open area. STAY AWAY from buildings, walls, and power poles

  • Stay AWAY from steep slopes which may be affected by landslides

  • If driving, STOP where it's safe and GET OUT. DO NOT cross bridges, overpasses or flyovers.
